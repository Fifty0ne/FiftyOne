    Our Values

    What we stand for

    Our company values define us. They act as a guiding set of beliefs to which we proudly hold ourselves to account. They ensure consistency across our team and in all the decisions that we make across all aspects of our business, relationships and our projects.

     

    Empower Success

    We thrive from the success of our partners and build our whole ethos around delivering the desired success, but also empowering our team to be the best that they can be.

    Passionate Experts

    We are passionate about what we do, and we do it well. We have built a team of experts in every area of our business and we can't wait to show you how passionate we are about your projects.

    Creative Problem Solvers

    We think outside the box. We make an effort to understand the purpose of an app, the potential pain points and the “why” behind something being built. This enables us to solve problems effectively.

    Your future, not just your career

    Here are some of the top reasons (aside from obvious benefits such as competitive salaries and retail discounts) that we believe you would like to know about working at The Distance.

    Work with amazing people

    A common misconception of development is that we work in isolation and are solely focused on writing code. In reality, we are highly collaborative professionals who work very closely with our partners to understand requirements and ensure the final product meets user needs. Working hard to be able to remain proud of everything we produce and what The Distance stands for.

    Be part of a refreshing culture who work hard and have fun

    Whether working closely with our teams on challenging projects, celebrating great work or partying together until the sun comes up, we all enjoy each other's company. There's usually a lot going on in our office from chocolate making classes, mini golf trips to delicious BBQs.

    We expect our people to question things and challenge lessons learnt, and we believe it’s this spirit that keeps employees interested, motivated and engaged. A high proportion of our people are here for the long term, and even when others have left for pastures new, a good number have found their way back to us.

    Drive your own development

    We're a growing company, and our employees need to grow with us. While the responsibility is on our employees to take the initiative with their learning and development, we will give you the tools and opportunities to nurture your talent and fulfil your potential. Whether this is through our role specific skills matrix development plans, or the chance to gain further professional qualifications. As a constantly growing and changing company, we want to give every one of our employees the opportunity to grow and change with us no matter what stage of their career they're at, whether that’s in their current role, taking the next step up or moving across the organisation to try something new.

    Enjoy a balanced life, inside and outside of work

    We understand that our employees have hobbies and commitments outside of work. Whether it's attending a school play, managing a lengthy commute, or taking care of personal appointments, we believe in giving our team the freedom to manage their own time. Our flexible working policy is designed to accommodate personal needs and ensure that work and life can harmoniously coexist.
